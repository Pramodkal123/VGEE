Application Process

A clear, structured 5-step enrolment process - designed for transparency and guidance from registration to travel.

  1. 1
    Online Registration
    Submit your application through our secure portal and confirm your preferred pathway.
  2. 2
    Structured Training Programme
    Begin pre-departure preparation or skill-based training aligned to your pathway.
  3. 3
    Partner Institution Acceptance
    Receive confirmation from an accredited European partner institution.
  4. 4
    Visa Processing & Approval
    Complete documentation and visa formalities with structured guidance.
  5. 5
    Travel & Programme Commencement
    Finalise travel arrangements and begin your international training journey.
