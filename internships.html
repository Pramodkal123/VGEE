WHAT IS AN INTERNSHIP PATHWAY?

VGEE facilitates access to structured internship pathways designed to complement education and vocational training. These internships provide practical exposure within regulated environments and are aligned with national training and education standards.

Internships coordinated through VGEE are:

  • Educational and skills-based
  • Time-bound and supervised

LEGAL & ETHICAL POSITIONING

VGEE operates strictly within education, training coordination, and advisory services.

  • We do not offer employment or recruitment services
  • We do not guarantee jobs or contracts
  • All training participation is voluntary and institution-led
  • Any employment outcomes depend on legal eligibility and independent employer decisions

This approach ensures compliance, transparency, and ethical engagement.
