Internship Programs

VGEE arranges internships for enrolled students and trainees. Internships provide hands-on experience, professional references and networking in Italy.

Eligibility

Open to students and trainees enrolled in VGEE pathway programs who hold the appropriate visa/permit to undertake internships in Italy.

Benefits

  • Real workplace experience
  • Industry contacts & references
  • Improved language and practical skills
Important: VGEE does NOT act as an international employment recruiter. Internship and work opportunities are provided only in Italy and via appropriate, registered channels.
