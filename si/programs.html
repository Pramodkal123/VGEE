Our Programs

VGEE offers diploma, vocational and one-year pathway programs designed to prepare candidates for study, internships and careers in Italy.

Diploma & Vocational Programs

Hospitality, culinary, caregiving, IT, fashion, mechanical trades and business studies — with application and arrival support.

One-Year Pathway Program

Intensive Italian language (A1–B1), vocational training and internship to improve employability in Italy.

Pre-departure & In-country Training

Pre-departure modules and in-country orientation focused on Italian work culture and safety standards.

Legal

VGEE is not a foreign employment agency. We do not recruit workers from Asia to overseas jobs. Employment support is only provided inside Italy via registered Italian agencies.
