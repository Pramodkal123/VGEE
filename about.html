Vertec Global Education & Employment (VGEE)

Bridging education, skills, and opportunity between Asia and Europe - responsibly and legally.

WHO WE ARE

Vertec Global Education & Employment (VGEE) is an international education, training coordination, and advisory organisation headquartered in Italy, with a focus on Asia-Europe education and skills pathways. VGEE was established to address the growing gap between Europe’s labour market needs and Asia’s emerging workforce potential through structured education, vocational training, and internship coordination - delivered in full compliance with national and European regulatory frameworks.

VGEE does not provide employment or recruitment services. Our work is strictly limited to education, training coordination, and institutional facilitation.

WHY VGEE WAS CREATED

European Union member states - particularly Italy - are facing structural demographic changes marked by aging populations, low fertility rates, and a shrinking domestic workforce. At the same time, many young people across Asia seek lawful, safe, and skill-based pathways to develop their futures abroad.

Current migration systems often fail to align education, skills development, and labour market demand, creating risks such as:

  • Labour shortages alongside informal employment
  • Skills mismatches
  • Worker exploitation and vulnerability
  • Social and economic strain on public systems

VGEE was created as a preventive, structured, and ethical response to these challenges - focusing on preparation, compliance, and long-term capability building, rather than irregular or unstructured migration.

WHAT WE DO

Our Core Activities

VGEE operates across four integrated areas:

  • Education Programme Coordination: Supporting access to recognised education programmes across Europe.
  • Vocational Training Pathways: Facilitating participation in structured skills development programmes aligned with national standards.
  • Internship Coordination: Enabling supervised, education-linked practical training experiences.
  • Advisory & Compliance Support: Providing guidance on eligibility, documentation, and institutional processes.

All services are delivered in collaboration with independent, accredited institutions and authorised partners.

WHAT MAKES VGEE DIFFERENT

Our Distinctive Approach

  • Education-First Approach: We prioritise structured education pathways rather than employment-driven migration.
  • Clear Programme Boundaries: Maintaining strict separation between training pathways and employment opportunities.
  • Transparent Legal Framework: Providing full clarity on legal limitations, rights, and obligations.
  • National Education Alignment: Ensuring programmes align with official national education systems.
  • Ethical & Risk-Aware Philosophy: Promoting responsible participation with informed decision-making.
  • Long-Term Skills Development Focus: Supporting sustainable career growth through recognised qualifications.

VGEE does not promise jobs, shortcuts, or guarantees. We focus on preparing people properly.

OUR MISSION

To facilitate structured, legal, and ethical education and training pathways that prepare individuals for participation in European societies while supporting workforce sustainability, institutional compliance, and social cohesion.

OUR VISION

To be recognised as a trusted coordination platform linking education, skills development, and policy-aligned mobility between Asia and Europe - contributing to safer migration practices, stronger labour market integration, and reduced reliance on informal systems.

LEADERSHIP & ACADEMIC FOUNDATION

VGEE is founded and led by professionals with backgrounds in human resource management, labour administration, and international risk management, supported by academic research and policy analysis.

The organisation’s strategy and operations are informed by ongoing doctoral research examining:

  • Labour shortages in Europe
  • Risks associated with unskilled and irregular migration
  • Education- and training-based risk mitigation models

This ensures VGEE’s work remains evidence-based, policy-aware, and forward-looking

ETHICAL & LEGAL COMMITMENT.

VGEE is committed to:

  • Legal compliance in all jurisdictions
  • Transparency with participants and partners
  • Ethical engagement and participant protection
  • Cooperation with public and private institutions

We reject misleading recruitment practices, informal labour arrangements, and any activity that undermines regulatory systems.
