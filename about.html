About VGEE

Vertec Global Education & Employment (VGEE) is a legally compliant and ethically driven education and mobility company. Our primary objective is to empower Asian students with European-standard training, skills, and international career pathways, providing structured guidance, resources, and long-term support.

Our secondary objective is to contribute to the European labour market by developing and connecting skilled, capable young professionals in alignment with industry needs, education standards, and regulatory frameworks.

Driving Purpose (Our Mission)

To bridge continents through education and opportunity, empowering Asian talent to thrive in global careers while strengthening European industries with skilled, dedicated professionals.

Shaping Tomorrow (Our Vision)

To be the leading bridge between Asian talent and European opportunity, recognised globally for transforming lives through ethical education, mobility, and internship solutions.

Why VGEE Is Unique

  • European-standard programmes aligned with international benchmarks
  • Seamless, guided mobility from training to internships
  • Ethical and legal compliance at every stage
  • Student-focused pathways designed around individual goals

Core Values in Action

  • Integrity: Transparent, accountable, and honest engagement
  • Excellence: Delivering high-quality training and outcomes
  • Innovation: Programmes aligned with evolving global needs
  • Empowerment: Building confidence and global capability

Trusted Partnerships

VGEE collaborates with recognised institutions across Europe and Asia to provide authentic educational experiences, professional networks, and international opportunities that support sustainable career development.

Legal and Ethical Compliance

VGEE strictly adheres to all education and mobility regulations to ensure programmes are safe, legal, and ethically delivered.

We do not operate as a foreign employment agency. Employment support is provided only within Italy through registered Italian agencies.
