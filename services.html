Essential Document & Administrative Support

VGEE ensures a smooth transition to life in Italy with comprehensive administrative guidance. We support international students, workers, and families with the key documents and processes required for legal identification, healthcare access, and everyday services.

  • Permesso di Soggiorno Assistance: Support for applying, renewing, and managing your residence permit.
  • Carta d’Identità Elettronica (CIE) Guidance: Help obtaining your electronic ID card for legal identification in Italy.
  • Tessera Sanitaria Registration: Secure your health card to access public healthcare services.
  • Codice Fiscale (Tax Code) Assistance: Obtain your tax code for banking, contracts, and compliance.
  • Bank Account Opening Support: Guidance on documentation and selecting suitable banking options.
